Common pricing questions

Quick answers.

Why is the snapshot really free? +
Because it's the fastest way for both of us to know if there's a fit. If we can help, you'll see it in the snapshot. If we can't, you've still got something useful — keep it, no questions. We'd rather not spend an hour on a sales call before we both know.
Are there any hidden costs? +
Two things you pay separately: domain registration (~₦15k/year) and hosting (~₦20k–₦60k/year depending on scale). We set everything up under your account, so you own it. Beyond that, the package price is the price.
If I buy the audit and then hire you to build, do I pay twice? +
No. Your ₦150,000 audit fee is credited in full against any build package (Redesign Lite and above) started within 90 days of receiving your report. The audit is a first step, not a separate expense — if the report convinces you to fix things, the money you spent understanding the problem goes toward solving it.
Can I pay in instalments? +
Default split is 50% on start, 50% on delivery. For Business Redesign and above we can split into three (40/30/30). We don't run financing partnerships — but we'll work with reasonable schedules in writing.
What if I just need one small thing? +
We'll quote hourly (₦25,000/hr for senior work) for anything that doesn't fit a package. Minimum 2 hours. If it ends up being more than ₦150k of hourly work, we'll suggest a package instead and credit the hourly toward it.
Do you work outside Nigeria? +
Yes, but selectively. We invoice in USD and pricing converts at the prevailing rate. Time zone differences mean we batch updates rather than running on-demand. Better fit for asynchronous-friendly clients.
