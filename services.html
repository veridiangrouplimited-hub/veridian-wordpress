What we do

Every service is built on one principle: honest work, openly measured.

Fourteen focused services — founder-led delivery, with specialists from the bench joining where the work calls for them. From quiet digital audits to AI advisory and agentic workflows. Pick what you need, or let us recommend a path after a free snapshot.

01 · DISCOVERY

Passive Digital Audit

A 360° look at your public online footprint — exactly what a customer (or competitor) sees from the outside. Public data only. No login, no penetration testing, no surprises.

  • Website performance & mobile UX
  • Google search visibility & local rankings
  • Google Business Profile completeness
  • Social media consistency & activity
  • Online reviews & reputation signals
  • Map listings & business directories
  • Brand information accuracy across the web
  • Competitive positioning snapshot
02 · WEBSITE

Website Redesign & Build

Custom websites that feel modern, load on Nigerian mobile networks, and turn visitors into enquiries. We build for thumbs first, desktops second.

  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Optimised for slow connections (3G/4G)
  • Built-in lead capture flows
  • WhatsApp & call-to-action integration
  • Bookings, menus, listings, payments — as needed
  • Accessible to WCAG AA standards
  • Editor handover so your team can update content
  • Domain, hosting & DNS setup included
03 · DISCOVERABILITY

SEO that earns rankings

We do the boring, technical work that actually moves the needle — not the spammy backlink schemes. Honest, sustainable, measurable.

  • On-page optimisation (titles, headings, schema)
  • Local SEO for Nigerian search terms
  • Site speed & Core Web Vitals fixes
  • Content strategy aligned to customer queries
  • Technical SEO (sitemaps, indexing, structured data)
  • Monthly ranking & traffic reports
  • Competitive keyword research
  • No black-hat shortcuts. Ever.
04 · LOCAL

Google Business Profile Optimisation

Most Nigerian SMEs leave 60% of their Google Business Profile blank. We claim, verify, and optimise it so you actually show up on Maps and local searches.

  • Profile claim & verification (we walk you through it)
  • Categories, services & attributes set correctly
  • Photo guidance & posting strategy
  • Review response templates & policy
  • Q&A seeding for common customer questions
  • Posts & offers calendar
  • Insights setup so you see who's calling
  • Multi-location support for chains
05 · BRAND

Online Brand Harmonisation

Your business name, address, and phone are different on five platforms. That's costing you trust and search rankings. We fix it, quietly and methodically.

  • NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)
  • Logo & visual identity across platforms
  • Bio & description harmonisation
  • Tone-of-voice mini-guidelines
  • Cover images & branded assets
  • Removal of outdated listings
  • Single source of truth document
  • Quarterly consistency check-ins
06 · WORDS

Content & Copy that converts

Words that sound like a human wrote them — for Nigerians, in Nigerian English where it fits. Clear, warm, professional.

  • Homepage & landing page copy
  • About, services, FAQ pages
  • Blog post writing & strategy
  • Product / service descriptions
  • Email templates & sequences
  • Social media content calendars
  • SEO-aware content briefs
  • Editing your team's drafts (training rate)
07 · PERFORMANCE

Site Speed & Performance Fixes

If your site takes 6 seconds to load on a phone in Lekki traffic, half your traffic is gone before you can speak. We make sites fast.

  • Image optimisation & lazy loading
  • Code minification & bundling
  • CDN setup for African edges
  • Lighthouse / PageSpeed Insights fixes
  • Database & query optimisation
  • Caching strategy & implementation
  • Mobile-network performance testing
  • Before/after benchmarks delivered

Analytics & Measurement Setup

If you can't measure it, you're guessing. We set up Google Analytics 4, Search Console, and a simple monthly dashboard you'll actually read.

  • Google Analytics 4 setup
  • Google Search Console setup
  • Goal & conversion tracking
  • WhatsApp click tracking
  • Form submission tracking
  • Monthly one-page report
  • Privacy-compliant analytics options
  • Team training on what to look at

Team Training & Handover

We don't want to be your forever web guy. We train your team to update content, respond to reviews, and run their own profiles.

  • 1-on-1 admin training (recorded)
  • Content update workflows
  • Review response training
  • Google Business posting training
  • Basic SEO hygiene checklist
  • Annotated runbook document
  • Quarterly refresher calls (optional)
  • Slack/WhatsApp support channel

Maintenance & Growth Retainer

Websites rot. CMSes update. Plugins break. Our monthly retainer keeps your site healthy, updated, and steadily improving — quietly.

  • Monthly security & CMS updates
  • Daily backups

  • Monthly content & SEO improvements

  • Priority bug-fix turnaround

Digital Strategy Advisory

Half-day or quarterly advisory sessions for founders who need a senior, honest second opinion on technology, vendors, or roadmap.

  • Vendor evaluation & negotiations
  • Technology roadmap reviews
  • Architecture & systems advice
  • Hiring & team structure input
  • M&A digital due diligence
  • Quarterly review retainers

Artificial Intelligence advisory & adoption

Help your business use AI tools sensibly — not as a hype experiment, but as a quiet advantage. We assess where AI actually helps, where it's overkill, and how to deploy it without inviting reputational or data risk.

  • Tool selection (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, local models)
  • AI-augmented workflow design
  • Data privacy & safety guardrails
  • Prompt engineering for non-technical staff
  • Vendor selection & cost benchmarking
  • Ethics, disclosure & acceptable-use policies

  • Quarterly review of model behaviour

Agentic AI workflows

Set up AI agents to handle the repetitive parts of running a business — customer inquiry triage, calendar coordination, document drafting, follow-ups — so your team focuses on the work that actually needs a human.

  • Agent design & deployment
  • Integration with WhatsApp, Gmail, CRMs, sheets
  • Human-in-the-loop checkpoints
  • Guardrails, logging & observability
  • Custom workflow agents per role
  • Performance & cost measurement
  • Plain-English operation logs your team can read

Practical, role-specific training for founders, managers, and frontline staff. From AI literacy to spreadsheet hygiene to confident self-management of your own systems — pitched at where your team actually is, not where a textbook says they should be.

  • Prompt-writing for non-technical staff
  • Safe AI use & data hygiene
  • Modern spreadsheets, docs & collaboration
  • Customer communication automation
  • Recorded sessions for new hires
  • Role-specific curricula (sales, ops, admin)
