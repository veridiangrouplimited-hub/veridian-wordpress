Case studies

Quiet work. Loud results.

A small selection of what happens when a Nigerian SME stops being invisible online. Real before/after scores. Real numbers. Real businesses (names lightly anonymised where requested).

Short-let · Lagos · Lekki Phase 1

A boutique short-let went from page 4 of Google to fully booked in 90 days.

The owner had a beautiful 3-bed apartment, great photos on Instagram, and zero direct bookings. We rebuilt the site, claimed and optimised the Google Business Profile, and ran a 12-week SEO sprint.

Before
38/100
  • Page 4 on "short-let lekki"
  • No Google Business listing
  • Mobile load: 6.2s
  • ~2 enquiries / week
→
After
86/100
  • Top 3 on 4 local terms
  • Verified Google profile · 47 reviews
  • Mobile load: 1.8s
  • ~22 enquiries / week
Law firm · Abuja · Maitama

A mid-sized law firm finally has a website partners aren't embarrassed to share.

10 partners, 22 lawyers, and a 2014-era website that loaded slowly and showed broken images. We rebuilt it as a credibility-first brochure with practice-area depth and partner profiles. No tricks — just a serious site for serious people.

Before
44/100
  • Site looked '10 years old' per partners
  • Broken image carousel
  • No partner bios
  • 0 enquiries from web in 12 mos
→
After
82/100
  • Refreshed brand & structure
  • Full partner directory + practice areas
  • PDF resource library
  • 9 enquiries in first month live
Secondary school · Port Harcourt

A private school doubled parent enquiries the term we fixed their Google profile.

The website was fine. The Google Business Profile was the problem — duplicate listings, wrong phone number, no posts since 2021, and a 2.8-star rating from old, unanswered reviews. We cleaned house.

Before
51/100
  • 3 duplicate Google listings
  • 2.8★ from 19 reviews (no responses)
  • Wrong phone number visible
  • ~12 enquiries / term
→
After
79/100
  • Single verified profile
  • 4.6★ from 41 reviews
  • Weekly posts + Q&A seeded
  • ~28 enquiries / term
Logistics startup · Lagos

A delivery startup ditched their bloated theme for a custom site that loads in under 2 seconds.

Their Shopify-derived theme had 47 plugins and a 9.2-second load time on 4G. We built a custom, lightweight site with WhatsApp ordering, tracking by phone, and a homepage that loads before you blink.

Before
41/100
  • 9.2s mobile load on 4G
  • Bounce rate: 78%
  • Plugin licences: ₦240k/year
  • WhatsApp not linked
→
After
88/100
  • 1.6s mobile load on 4G
  • Bounce rate: 31%
  • Plugin licences: ₦0/year
  • Click-to-WhatsApp on every page
